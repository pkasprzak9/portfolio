About me

Hello, I'm Paweł, a 23-year-old Computer Science student deeply passionate about technology, astrophysics, and the profound art of problem-solving through programming and database management. When I'm not immersed in the world of code, you'll often find me exploring a rich tapestry of music, from the timeless classics of old rock to the rhythms of hip-hop, jazz, funk, soul, and R&B. My curiosity extends beyond technology and music. I'm also captivated by the mysteries of astrophysics and the vastness of cosmology, as well as the intricate workings of the human mind in the field of psychology.

In this ever-evolving digital age, I'm driven by a singular goal: to seamlessly combine my technical expertise with a profound sense of empathy. I aspire to craft innovative, human-centric solutions that not only address complex challenges but also resonate with the people they serve. My trusty golden retriever serves as a constant reminder of the importance of empathy in our interactions with technology. While my future lies in the realm of machine learning and artificial intelligence, the specific path is still unfolding. My approach to problem-solving is guided by honesty, logic, and well-supported opinions.
